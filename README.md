# 📊 Superstore Strategic Insights Dashboard – Power BI Project

A comprehensive Power BI business intelligence solution designed to analyse and visualise key performance metrics from the Global Superstore dataset. This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights to support strategic retail decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This end-to-end Power BI project addresses key business challenges around **profitability, customer behaviour, and operational performance**.
It delivers a structured analytics solution across **seven dashboard pages** and a consolidated **Executive Summary**, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance and identify optimisation opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Despite strong sales performance, retail operations often face **profitability challenges driven by discounting, customer behaviour, and regional inefficiencies**.
This project aims to identify the underlying drivers of revenue and profit to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories
* Analyse high-value customer segments and regions
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability
* Discover sales seasonality trends over time
* Profile the top 10 customers by sales and profit
* Assess geographic performance at state and city level

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

* 📌 **Executive Summary** – One-page strategic overview of KPIs and insights
* 🛒 **Product Performance** – Category-level sales and profit analysis
* 👥 **Customer Segments** – Segment and regional profitability breakdown
* 💸 **Discount Impact** – Relationship between discounting and profit
* 🏆 **Top 10 Customers** – Revenue contribution and behavioural insights
* 📅 **Sales Trends** – Monthly and quarterly seasonality patterns
* 🌍 **Geo Performance** – State and city-level performance insights

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Identified **profit leakage driven by discounting (~15.6%)**, negatively impacting margins
* High-value customer segments contribute a **significant share of total revenue (~35%)**, indicating strong concentration
* Certain product categories generate high sales but **low profitability**, highlighting inefficiencies
* Seasonal trends reveal **peak sales periods**, supporting demand planning and forecasting

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimise discount strategy to improve profit margins
* Prioritise high-margin products and reduce reliance on low-profit items
* Implement targeted retention strategies for high-value customers
* Focus on underperforming regions to improve overall business performance
