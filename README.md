## Overview

This project delivers a **production-ready business intelligence solution** designed to analyse retail performance across sales, profitability, customer behaviour, and geographic trends.
It transforms raw transactional data into **structured insights that support strategic decision-making and performance optimisation**.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organisations often experience strong revenue growth while facing **profitability challenges driven by discounting strategies, product mix inefficiencies, and regional performance gaps**.
This project identifies key drivers of revenue and profit to enable **data-driven optimisation of business performance**.

---

## Objectives

* Analyse category and sub-category performance across sales and profit
* Identify high-value customers and revenue concentration patterns
* Evaluate the impact of discounting on profitability
* Detect sales seasonality and temporal trends
* Assess regional and state-level performance disparities

---

## Solution

Designed and implemented an **end-to-end Power BI analytics system** comprising:

* 8 interactive dashboard pages and an executive summary
* Star schema data model for efficient querying and reporting
* DAX-driven KPI framework for dynamic performance tracking
* Interactive visualisations enabling drill-down and user-driven analysis

## Key Insights

* Discounting (~15.6%) is a primary driver of **profit margin erosion**
* High-value customers contribute ~35% of revenue, indicating **revenue concentration risk**
* Several product categories exhibit **high sales but low profitability**
* Seasonal trends highlight **peak demand periods**, supporting forecasting and planning
* Regional disparities reveal **underperforming markets requiring optimisation**

---

## Business Recommendations

* Optimise discount strategy to improve margin performance
* Prioritise high-margin products and reduce reliance on low-profit items
* Implement targeted retention strategies for high-value customers
* Focus on underperforming regions to improve overall profitability
